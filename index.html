ACES (Associaltion of Computer Engineerings Students ) is a association that has been working for the uplifting the skills of computer engineering students of Purwanchal engineering campus , Dharan . ACES is a non-political and non-profitable association that works only and only for the purpose of computer engineering students . ACES is working not only to develop the skill of students but also has helped in improving the condition of the computer engineering faculty by different purposes.
